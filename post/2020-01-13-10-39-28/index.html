2019

2019 is the year that my marriage broke up.

2019 is the year that my daughter no longer woke up in the same house as me every day.

2019 is the year that I lost my job.

2019 is the year that Mom found out she had breast cancer.

2019 is the year that Dad found out he had fibrosis in his lungs.

Fuck 2019.
