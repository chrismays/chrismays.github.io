Hope Self Loathing

Hope for a Self-Loathing Kid I’ve always loved music, but I can pinpoint exactly when music became “more” for me. In 1984, when I was 13, my Aunt Pat was visiting my family in Columbus, GA and she and my Mom were going out shopping. Aunt Pat knew I liked music and offered to purchase me a 45. I told her I wanted either [Love Somebody by Rick Springfield] or [Dancing in the Dark by Bruce Springsteen].
